#  Telco Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn using the Telco Customer Churn dataset. The goal is to understand why customers leave and evaluate the financial impact of churn on the business.

The project is divided into two main parts:

* **Business Analysis** → understanding customer behavior
* **Financial Analysis** → measuring revenue impact

---

## Objectives

* Identify key factors that drive customer churn
* Analyze customer behavior patterns
* Estimate revenue lost due to churn
* Provide actionable business recommendations

## Business Analysis Insights

### Churn Rate

* Around **26.6% of customers churn**
* Indicates a significant retention problem

### Contract Type

* **Month-to-month customers churn the most**
* Long-term contracts (1–2 years) improve retention

### Payment Method

* **Electronic check users have the highest churn**
* Automatic payments reduce churn risk

### Tenure

* New customers are more likely to churn
* Long-term customers are more loyal

 **Key Insight:**
Early-stage customers are the most vulnerable → onboarding is critical.

---

## Financial Analysis Insights

### Revenue Impact

* A significant portion of monthly revenue comes from customers who churn
* This represents **direct financial loss**

### Revenue Stability

* Revenue from long-term contracts is more stable
* Short-term customers create financial volatility

### High-Risk Segments

* Month-to-month customers
* Electronic check users
* New customers

  **Key Insight:**
Reducing churn = protecting revenue.

## Key Business Recommendations

* Encourage customers to switch to **long-term contracts**
* Promote **automatic payment methods**
* Improve **early customer experience (first 3–6 months)**
* Target high-risk customers with retention strategies

---

## Conclusion

Customer churn is not only a customer behavior problem but also a **financial risk**.

By focusing on retention strategies in high-risk segments, the company can:

* Reduce churn
* Increase revenue stability
* Improve long-term growth
